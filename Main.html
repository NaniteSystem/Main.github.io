History

DUDES is a metaverse in each of which one very large family lives.
DUDES sorts of creatures live in these universes, from small plankton to unnatural, unhappy, but sweet and friendly ghosts. In one case, there are more than 10 in existence. One of our goals is to create a VR game where you can live with unlimited possibilities, make friends and have fun. In this VR game you will live where you feel comfortable, our friends can help you get used to their metaverse. When you become friends with them, they will give you access to their market, where you can buy the things you wanted. These things are used by themselves, especially for you.
